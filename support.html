Help for saving receipts and reports

Using the app

Add a receipt with the merchant, date, category, payment method, total, tax, and notes. Receipt Buddy automatically includes it in the matching monthly report.

Monthly reports

Choose a month to review totals, spending by category, daily spend, top merchants, and payment split. Use CSV export when you need a copy for bookkeeping.

Deleting receipts

Open the saved receipts table, find the receipt, and use the delete button in that row. The monthly report refreshes after the receipt is removed.

Contact

If you need help with Receipt Buddy, email receiptbuddy.support@gmail.com.
